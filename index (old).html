Brand identity design for Upper Crust Bakery

The branding identity of Upper Crust Bakery captures the bakery's vision of being an inviting and homely place for people of all ages to visit. Through a warm color palette, rough lines and texture, vintage-like font, and imagery of a fresh loaf of bread served on a platter, the designs exhibit a vivid presentation of the bakery's long, established history and amiable presence. The carefully designed logo and cohesive pieces of the business card and letterhead were crafted in Illustrator and made print-ready with bleed, printer's marks, and CMYK color format.
