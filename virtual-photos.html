Virtual Photography: A form of media art revolving around screenshots of video games

Virtual photography - how and why?

Virtual photography is achieved using the in-game photomode, a feature in video games that pauses the game and allows gamers to control the camera to take a screenshot under the right conditions. Depending on the game, the photomode can offer a various tools to further enhance the quality of the photo, such as depth of field, brightness and contrast, tint and saturation, and so forth.

Virtual photography provides one with the opportunity to not only exercise compostional techniques to weave an intriguing visual story but also capture a dramatic scene that might not be achievable in reality.
